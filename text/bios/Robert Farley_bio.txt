Over the course of my adult life, I have lived in Ohio, New York, Indiana and Florida. I have also travelled from coast to coast with my camera in tow the whole time. What started as a hobby and personal passion has turned into something more. I now display my work at The ARTery at Front Street. I display at multiple shows across the state of Ohio. And, I have recently won 2nd Place for Booth at the Duveneck Art Show, First Place for Amateur Photography at The Tipp City Art Exhibit, and Second Place at The Piqua Art Exhibition.
