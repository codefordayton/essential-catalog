I am a member of The Contemporary Gallery (formerly DVAC), Tri-Pod Camera Club, Professional Photographers of America. Also, Piqua Arts Council and Dayton Society of Artists. My work has been displayed and sold in various shows throughout the Miami Valley. I have won an award for Best of Show, Amateur, in " Light Up Dayton" in 2009 and 2012, First Place in Photography at Piqua Art Council's multi-media show in 2019 and Second Place in Photography at Troy Hayner Cultural Center's "Through Your Eyes" photography show. I recently received a First Place in the MAC Art show in 2023 in the People Category.
