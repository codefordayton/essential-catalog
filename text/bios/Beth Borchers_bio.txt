If you gaze deep within each piece, you will be forever changed by the soul reaching artwork of Beth Borchers! From a young age, Beth's love for nature, drawing and belief in a world of magic was evident. These themes are reflected in her artwork. Beth's paintings are captivating and distinctive, each one telling a story, respective to each individual. Her style is original, abstract, and is meant to connect you to your heart. Beth is a self-taught mixed media artist who has taken the art world by storm with her intense colors and bold, sometimes playful choices. She loves adding splashes, swirls and circles of color or metallic accents giving each piece another layer of interest and depth. You never know what you may see if you contemplate it long enough. That is the magic! Beth’s art is more than just visually stunning - it mirrors her deep understanding of the power of love, peace, and joy. She strives to create a stirring of emotion in all who view it. Her work reflects her life, her experiences, and the beauty she has chosen to see in the world around her.
