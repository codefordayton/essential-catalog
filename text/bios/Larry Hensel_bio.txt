Larry Hensel is a graduate of the Dayton Art Institute with an associate degree in graphic design. He has worked as a graphic designer for over 30 years with advertising agencies and industrial companies. Larry also has his own freelance graphic design business. Larry is currently employed by the Greene County Convention & Visitors Bureau as their in-house graphic designer. Along with graphic design duties for the CVB, Larry is also in charge of documenting events using photography. The photos become part of the CVB library and are used for future promotional campaigns. In his spare time, Larry likes to get out and photograph historical architecture, car shows, and local and state parks. Larry has used many of the photos he has taken to create an extensive portfolio of architectural element panels, poster designs for vintage aircraft, and posters for historical buildings.
