Nancy Shuler is a full-time artist working in Troy, Ohio, north of Dayton. She received her BFA from Wright State University. She studied in Tuscany at La Macina di San Cresci, Greve, Italy. Shuler has been widely recognized in the Dayton area and has exhibited at the Dayton Art Institute, Front Street Galleries, and APAC Performing Arts Center. She has been involved in Art in the City through the Downtown Dayton Partnership. During her time as Interim Director of the Dayton Artist Society, Nancy taught art. Her work has been widely recognized in the Dayton area, and her passion for art is lifelong. Nancy combines color, texture, and design elements to create stunning pieces of art.
