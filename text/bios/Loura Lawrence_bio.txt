Loura is a travel (tourism) and nature photographer plus digital artist and jewelry designer/maker, based in Dayton, Ohio. She was trained in photojournalism at Ohio University, Athens, is an alumna with Honors of Sinclair Community Community College, holds a BA in English magna cum laude from New York Institute of Technology, and has over 20 years of photography experience. Loura's work has been featured multiple times in "Creation Illustrated Magazine," "Mother Earth Magazine," PC-Goenner Talent Agency, Kirkmont Presbyterian Church, Five Rivers MetroParks Publications, Dayton Society of Art, and Dayton Focus Photo Club. Loura is a proud member of Dayton Tripod Camera Club, The Dayton Society of Art, Dayton Focus Photo Club, and Preservation Dayton, Inc.
