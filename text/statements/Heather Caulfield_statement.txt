My name is Heather Caulfield. I am a soulful creative visionary Artist, Art Director, Teacher, Author, Designer and Editor. I have painted since I can remember, art has always been a best friend and intuitive messenger, my paintings speak to me, sometimes years later delivering messages to my soul. My love of the renaissance era led me to study many masters such as Michaelangelo and Davinci and other classic painters. They influenced my traditional approach to painting techniques and my love of figurative works and portraiture. Now a mother to a budding artist, I continue figurative studies while reconnecting with my ancestral roots, exploring unique creative expressions ineffably and beyond. I am a spiritual dreamer and infinite conduit of pure source, downloading and documenting information from the unseen and assisting creation since birth. Lover of Nature, whimsical expression and all things mysterious. Experiencer of mystical happenings, delivering a voice for the collective to acknowledge the magic that Nature is. A spiritually charged terrestrial, deep in wonderous imagination, often caught in a daydream or away with the fairies in all delight. Forever a student, studying and seeking universal truths and divine wisdom. A Neptunian painter of innate emotion, capturing the soul on canvas, the pure essence of one’s being. I honor the infinite soul and acknowledge the infinite ONE in you!
