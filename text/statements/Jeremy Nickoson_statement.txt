I have specialized in landscape and nature photography, with some dabbling in astrophotogray. I do my best to make sure what comes straight off the camera is as inspiring as it can be. My goal with photography is to help encourage others to get outside and experience the amazing wonders that are just waiting to be found in nature. Most of my prints are printed on metal (aluminum) or metallic paper, which give an incredible vibrancy and character regular photo prints don't have.
