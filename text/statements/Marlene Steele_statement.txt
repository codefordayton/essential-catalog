I work as a painter, utilizing vibrant and daring colors. I begin without a specific plan, allowing the painting to guide its direction. I've noticed that my present state of mind influences various changes in my artwork. The unpredictability of the process excites me, as each moment is a discovery, a new adventure waiting to unfold. Through my work, I strive to inspire others to embrace the beauty of unpredictability, to find joy in the process of creation, and to celebrate the unique essence that makes each of us a masterpiece in our own right.
