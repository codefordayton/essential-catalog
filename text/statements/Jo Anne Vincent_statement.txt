Hello ! My name is Jo Anne Vincent and I have been creating jewelry for 13 years.
I am intrigued by the myriad of possibilities in combining color, pattern & texture.
My sterling silver and copper jewelry begins with selecting the beads... pearl, beaded beads from Bali or hand-blown glass from California, agate, turquoise, opal and lapis to name a few.
I have always had an eye for design and color and the desire to be different and unique - to stand alone. For me, being involved in creating jewelry provides an amazing outlet for the expression of my artistic vision.
