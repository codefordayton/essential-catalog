My creative endeavor revolves around the exploration of sensory perception and the boundaries of reality. Through my art, I strive to evoke a state of hallucination without the use of drugs, inviting viewers to embark on a journey of visual transcendence. By employing various mediums and techniques, I aim to challenge conventional notions of perception and awaken a heightened sense of awareness within the observer. Each piece serves as a portal to an alternate reality, where the mind is free to roam and imagination knows no bounds.
